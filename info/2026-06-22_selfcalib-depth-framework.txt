GOAL (user)
  Framework that learns metric distance from a SINGLE camera image, using
  synchronized LiDAR as ground-truth depth, where the model also learns/tunes
  the camera parameters.

SCOPING DECISIONS (user-selected)
  - Calibration: FULL self-calibration (learn f, c, distortion; minimal priors).
  - Backbone:    FINE-TUNE a foundation model (Depth Anything V2 / Metric3D).
  - Target:      CAMERA-AWARE / generalize across focal lengths & cameras.

CORE IDEA
  The three pieces couple through a per-pixel RAY MAP:
    calibration theta -> ray map -> conditions DAv2 decoder -> depth-along-ray
    -> back-project with theta -> 3D points -> compared to metric LiDAR.
  A wrong focal length => back-projected points don't match LiDAR => gradient
  flows back into theta. LiDAR replaces multi-view bundle adjustment as the
  geometric anchor (DroidCalib idea, re-grounded on LiDAR).
