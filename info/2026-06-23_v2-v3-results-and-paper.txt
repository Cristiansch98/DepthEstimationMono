KEY FINDINGS
  - Self-calibration works across ALL versions (<=1.4% focal err on held-out
    cameras). v3 best: fx 0.26% / fy 0.41%, cx/cy sub-pixel. CORE CLAIM HOLDS.
  - NEGATIVE RESULT (pinpointed): camera-conditioned global-affine scale head
    (P-A) underperformed the free conv head on depth. v2 regressed (wrong A2:
    used log-depth; DAv2 disparity is affine in INVERSE-depth). v2b fixed the
    functional form (1/Z = s*disp + t) -> recovered most of it (0.249) but still
    lost to v1. A single global affine is too rigid for per-pixel metric depth.
  - REAL LEVER = unfreezing the DAv2 backbone (P-B). v3 nearly halved AbsRel
    (0.204->0.112), d1 0.71->0.88, best calibration, best far-range vehicle dist.
  - Bounded radial distortion (P-C) enabled in v2/v3 (k=0.05*tanh), learned
    k1~0.0068 on front_center; stable, no NaN.
