FINDING (honest, important — NOT a bug)
  The framework runs unchanged on every dataset, but does NOT zero-shot transfer.
  Structural cause (model.py): theta = latent[cam_idx] + 0.1*delta_head(feat).
  The per-camera latent (AV2-learned focal) dominates; the image-conditioned
  residual is only 0.1x and zero-initialized, so on an unseen camera the model
  predicts ~the AV2 focal. KITTI true fx~301 (518-space) is far from the AV2 front
  latent ~595 -> 97% fx error and collapsed depth scale (d1 0.02). nuScenes ~410
  is closer -> less bad (45% / d1 0.19). The depth head's metric scale is focal-
  conditioned, so wrong theta also degrades depth.

  TWO limitations exposed (both actionable):
   (1) metric depth unchanged — depth head (ScaleMappingHead) frozen & AV2-tuned, so

   (2) fy under-recovers (10-13%) because loss_prior has an aspect penalty
       (log fx/fy)^2 that is wrong once resized to 518^2 -> pulls fy toward fx.
