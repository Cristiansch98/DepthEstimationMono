GOAL (user)
  Copy /home/cubos/Frame2BEV/d57wHsiTS0E.mp4 into the project, split to frames at a
  light resolution, and make the same visualizations: depth heatmap, direct video,
  and a BEV gif (tracking cars), using the best model (UniDepth-V2-L).

DATA
  Source: 1280x720, 30 fps, ~929 s (27,883 frames) highway dashcam (own red hood +
  timestamp overlay at bottom). Copied to data/realvideo/d57wHsiTS0E.mp4.
  Clip: t=30 s, 30->6 fps (every 5th frame), 72 frames, downscaled 768x432 (pyav;
  no ffmpeg locally) -> data/realvideo/frames/.

PIPELINE  (src/calib_depth/video_demo.py; baselines venv: unidepth + torchvision)
  - UniDepth-V2-L -> metric depth + self-predicted intrinsics (no GT/LiDAR/calib).
  - torchvision Faster R-CNN (COCO) -> car/truck/bus 2-D boxes (no extra install).
  - cars back-projected (box bottom-centre + depth + predicted K) -> BEV; online
    constant-velocity Kalman tracker (greedy NN assoc + gating) for time-consistency.
  Outputs viz_video/{heatmap,direct,bev}.gif (72 frames, 600 px, 6 fps).

KEY FINDING (ties to the paper)
  UniDepth's ABSOLUTE metric scale fails badly on this OOD dashcam: predicted road
  ahead ~223 m (depth range 157-1306 m, median 440) vs true ~10-30 m -- ~40x too
  large (pred fx~402, plausible; the scale, not the focal, is wrong). Its RELATIVE
  depth structure is fine. Fixes for the visualizations:
   - heatmap: per-frame RELATIVE normalization (p2-p98) -> clean near->far structure.
   - BEV: ground-plane metric-scale recovery (assume camera height 1.5 m; the road
     strip below horizon / above hood should sit at Y=h_cam) -> cars land at sensible
     5-15 m. This is monocular scale recovery, GT-free.

RESULT
  heatmap.gif: relative depth (road blue near, vehicles cyan mid, sky red far).
  direct.gif : frames + Faster R-CNN boxes with persistent track ids.
  bev.gif    : top-down cars (~5-15 m ahead) with Kalman-smoothed tracks + trails.

UPDATE — MP4 export + per-car distance labels
  Outputs are now MP4 (cv2.VideoWriter, mp4v codec; no ffmpeg needed) and far lighter
  than the GIFs: heatmap 5MB->0.8MB, direct 7.5MB->2.5MB, bev 0.78MB->0.44MB (720px,
  6 fps). Per-car DISTANCE labels added: direct frame boxes show "#id <dist>m"; BEV
  markers show "#id <dist> m" (dist = Euclidean BEV range, scale-recovered). Example
